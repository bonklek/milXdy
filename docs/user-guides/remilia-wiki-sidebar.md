# Remilia Wiki Sidebar User Guide

Remilia Wiki opens Remilia Wiki pages in the shared side rail so you can read references without leaving X/Twitter.

## Where To Find It

- Open X/Twitter.
- Click **Wiki** in the milXdy side rail.
- Click inline wiki links or preview read-more links to route them into the sidebar.

## Common Tasks

- Pin or unpin **Remilia Wiki** from the Apps Hub.
- Use the sidebar open-in-tab control when you want the full wiki page.
- Modifier-click a wiki link when you prefer the browser's normal new-tab behavior.
- Use **Link later** from the X/Twitter context menu to save selected phrases for future wiki work.
- Use **Create Wiki entry with Grok** from the context menu when drafting a new Remilia Wiki article prompt.

## Notes

The sidebar accepts validated Remilia Wiki URLs only. It stores the most recent wiki URL and sidebar placement locally.
