# Remilia Wiki Article Patterns

These patterns are based on sampled Remilia Wiki pages including `Milady Maker`, `Charlotte Fang`, `Vibe Shift`, `$CULT`, `Remilia Corporation`, and `Beetle Wiki`.

## Common Structure

- Many full articles begin with `{{SHORTDESC:...}}`.
- Work-in-progress or short pages may include `{{Article quality|stub}}` or `{{Article quality|wip}}`.
- Major entities often use an infobox:
  - `{{Infobox person}}` for people.
  - `{{Infobox organization}}` for collectives or companies.
  - `{{Infobox NFT collection}}` for NFT collections.
  - `{{Infobox website}}` for websites and documentation projects.
- The opening paragraph usually starts with bold page title syntax: `'''Page Title''' is ...`.
- Strong pages establish the topic, date or period, relationship to Remilia, and why it is notable in the first 1-2 paragraphs.
- References are collected with `<references />`.

## Style Notes

- Use neutral, specific language: "is a", "was launched", "emerged in", "is associated with".
- Prefer exact dates when known. Use date ranges for scenes, eras, or movements.
- Link related pages on first mention, not every mention.
- Avoid assuming insider knowledge. Define scene terms briefly.
- Treat controversy carefully: attribute claims and cite sources.
- Keep a first draft modest if sourcing is thin. A short accurate stub is better than a long unsupported article.
